Cookies are great

Ingredients

Click here for a list of ingredients
  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cut light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  • Mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder
  • In a separate bowl, mix butter and sugars until well combined
  • Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy
  • Mix in dry ingredients until well combined
  • Add chocolate chips and mix well
  • Roll 2 TBS of dough into balls and place on baking pan
  • Bake for 8-10 minutes
